CONCLUSION
================================================================================

GlobalBank International Inc. maintains a strong compliance posture with an 
overall compliance score of 94.7%, representing a 2.4 percentage point 
improvement from Q3 2024. All critical regulatory requirements including 
Basel III capital ratios, LCR, and SOX internal controls are met or exceeded.

Key strengths include robust capital buffers, effective internal controls, 
and strong liquidity management. Areas requiring attention include wholesale 
funding concentration, manual AML processing backlogs, and certain GDPR 
third-party management processes.

Management has committed to addressing all identified deficiencies with 
targeted remediation plans and appropriate resource allocation.
